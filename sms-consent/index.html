SMS Consent for Verification Codes

Last Updated: December 9, 2025

When you enter your phone number in the Tullu app or website and tap "Send code", you are requesting that we send you a one-time SMS text message to verify your identity.

By using this feature, you agree that:

Tullu may send you one-time passcodes (OTP) and other account-security messages by SMS to the mobile number you provide.

Messages are transactional only and are used for sign-up, login, password reset, and sensitive account changes.

We do not use this number for marketing or promotional SMS without your separate consent.

Message & data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Opt-out & help

You can stop receiving SMS from Tullu at any time by replying STOP to any message.

For help, reply HELP, email us at support@tulluapp.com, or visit our Help Center.

For more details on how we handle your information, please see our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
