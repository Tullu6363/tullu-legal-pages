Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the Tullu app from your app store and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide basic information and verify your email or phone number.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions to reset your password via email or SMS.

How do I update my account information?

Go to your profile settings in the app to update your personal information, contact details, and preferences.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the settings menu. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
